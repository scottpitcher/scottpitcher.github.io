Intro

Thank you for taking the time to visit my website portfolio! Here I've laid out some of my favourite past projects, and what I'm currently working on/discovering!


I'm a ML Engineer with 2+ years of experience, specializing in reinforcement learning and natural language processing. I graduated from the University of South Florida with a Bachelors of Science in Mathematics with a focus in both Data Analysis and Business Intelligence. Within Data Science, I've found myself most interested in the applications of AI and the development of language models. Outside of my professional background, I love cooking, taking a walk with music, and constantly challenging myself in the gym; I am currently located in New York City.

Below you can find my personal projects that I completed in my personal time; while each project is unique, their shared goal has always been to challenge myself!
